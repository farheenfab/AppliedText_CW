4:24 The way Hoyeon was gesturing towards Mr. Jung-jae using only one hand but then quickly supported her raised hand with the other one when she realized it  Even he cracked up!








For people who may not know, it is a rule of the Korean etiquette to support one hand with the other while doing gestures that may not require the usage of both hands (like shaking hands) to exhibit respect towards one’s seniors/ elders, or at times, even people whom you’re of so familiar with.
